Application & Dosage (For All Crops)

250g
per 1.0 acre of land
Or according to crop and soil condition.
Suitable for all crops via flood, drip, or drenching irrigation.
Method: Mix 250g with 10 litres of water, let it dissolve for 3 hours. After that pour into 100–200 litres of water and apply immediately using flood, drip, or drenching irrigation.

If the soil has enough moisture, use this product by broadcasting method — combine with sand or organic manure before broadcasting.
