Application & Dosage (For All Crops)

1kg
per 1–2 acres
Or as per soil condition, crops and stage of crops.
Suitable for foliar, drip, drench, flood & broadcasting application.
Foliar Application: First dissolve the product in 20L fresh water and use 50–100 ml/L for foliar application.

Soil Application: Dissolve the product in 200L fresh water and apply through Drip, Drench and Flood irrigation — also through broadcasting as per soil condition, crops and stages of crops.
