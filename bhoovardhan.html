About SP-Bhoovardhan

⚗️

Six Essential Micronutrients in One

SP-Bhoovardhan supplies all six required essential micronutrients — Zinc, Iron, Manganese, Copper, Boron and Molybdenum — together in an optimum dose to crop, preventing mixed micronutrient deficiencies occurring at different growth stages.

⚡

EDTA Chelated for Fast Absorption

SP-Bhoovardhan is chelated with EDTA and thus assures fast absorption of all individual micro elements into plants — ensuring quick availability and maximum uptake efficiency across all crop types.
