Benefits

🌿

Organic Enzyme Complex for All Crops

SP Ecozyme+ acts as an organic enzyme complex of roots, leaves, flowers & fruits on every kind of crop. Also suitable for vegetable, fruit crops and all types of field crops.

🌸

Prevents Micronutrient Deficiencies & Improves Flowering

Helps prevent micronutrient deficiencies in different forms occurring gradually at different growth stages. Immediately absorbed by the root system — induces more flowers, prevents flower dropping, and avoids fruit dropping.
